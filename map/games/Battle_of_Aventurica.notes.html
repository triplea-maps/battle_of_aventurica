============================================

The dark wizard and demon master BORBARAD
was defeated years ago and has been sent
to the limbo.

Now some of his followers fetched him back
during a magic ritual.

Again he tries to conquer Aventuria and
this time he is supported by Orcs and
Al Anfa a country in the deep south.

===================GOALS====================

Capture 5 of the 8 captial countries
or kill 3 of the 5 opponent leaders

================TURN ORDER=================

Imperial - Orcs - Thorwal - Borbarad - Al Anfa
